Measurement Guide

Front Chainline Measurements

  1. Seatpost diameter (SP): Measure the diameter of the seatpost using vernier calipers.
  2. Chainring set width (CR): Measure the total width of the front chainring set (all chainrings together) using vernier calipers.
  3. Distance from seatpost to end of largest chainring (D): Measure the distance from the seatpost to the outer edge of the largest chainring. Use a ruler or calipers.

Rear Chainline Measurements

  1. Hub width (a) - OLD: Measure the distance between the locknuts on the rear hub (over locknut distance).
  2. Distance from hub locknut to beginning of smallest sprocket (b): Measure from the hub locknut to the face of the smallest sprocket.
  3. Width from smallest to largest sprocket (c): Measure the total width from the smallest to the largest sprocket on the cassette.
